## Visit to Ambuja Cement Manufacturing Plant, Ropar With First Year Students

GNDEC School of Architecture's first-year students got hands-on experience during an educational site visit to Ambuja Cement Manufacturing Plant at Ropar. Students were provided with an opportunity to visit the CCR Room, Physical Testing Lab, Chemical Testing Lab, Bag Testing & XRF Lab, Packing Plant, Mill Area, and Raw Material Section to understand the complete process from the delivery of raw materials to the dispatch of finished products. Students gained practical insights into large-scale industrial operations and Enhanced understanding of sustainable practices employed in modern cement manufacturing. A heartfelt thanks to Er Varinder Kumar Punj and Ar Balbir Bagga for helping get approvals from the management of Adani. We acknowledge Prof Vivek Sehgal and Ar. Shivani Garg for coordinating this visit.

## Visit to Atam Vallabh Janpath, Ludhiana With First Year Students

GNDEC School of Architecture's first-year students got hands-on experience during a live construction site visit to Atam Vallabh Janpath, Ludhiana.

The visit provided valuable insights into foundation work, brick masonry, formwork, and steel reinforcement detailing, enabling students to connect classroom concepts with on-site practices. They also explored the design of a luxurious villa spanning 2,200 sq. yards, with a focus on spatial planning at a larger scale.

A heartfelt thanks to Project Architect Ar. Sanjay Goel and Structural Consultant Er. B.S. Chahal for welcoming us to their site and sharing their expertise with our budding architects. We acknowledge Prof Vivek Sehgal for coordinating this visit.

## Seminar-Cum-Discussion Session On “From Concept To Critique: Navigating The Architectural Thesis Journey” By Ar. Pappal Suneja

GNDEC School of Architecture organized a seminar-cum-discussion session on “From Concept to Critique: Navigating the Architectural Thesis Journey” by Ar. Pappal Suneja, Ph.D. Scholar, Bauhaus Universität Weimar, Germany, for the final-year B.Architecture students.

Ar. Suneja threw light on key aspects of the thesis process — from topic selection to the development of the final design solution. He also interacted with the students, discussing their individual areas of interest, offering valuable critiques, and guiding them on how to advance effectively in their thesis journey.

Overall, it was an informative and engaging session that provided valuable insights for the students. Ar. Akanksha Sharma, Head- GNDEC School of Architecture, thanked the resource person for his contribution and encouraged the students to continue striving for excellence in their thesis work.

## EVENTS

## NEXUS Event – NASA (National Association Of Students of Architecture, India)

GNDEC School of Architecture (SOA), Ludhiana successfully organised a one-day NEXUS event on 21st February 2026, bringing together around 100 students from various colleges and universities across Punjab for a rich academic and creative exchange. Hosted at the GNDEC School of Architecture, the event featured two engaging sessions: the “Paint on Canvas” workshop, which encouraged students to create architectural masterpieces on canvas pouches through hands-on artistic exploration, and “Harmonize Your Space: Vastu Principles for Modern Living”, an insightful lecture by Vikas Aggarwal (Aura Expert) focusing on the basics of Vastu Shastra, spatial harmony, and practical tips for homes and workspaces. Organised under the NEXUS platform in association with the National Association of Students of Architecture, the event fostered creativity, cultural awareness, and interdisciplinary learning, providing students from across Punjab a shared platform to interact, learn, and celebrate architecture beyond the classroom in Ludhiana.
